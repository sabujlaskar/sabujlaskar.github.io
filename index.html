About Me

I am a PhD student at Texas A&M University, working with Professor Eun Jung Kim in the High Performance Computing Laboratory. My research area is computer architecture and systems. Specifically, I work on machine learning accelerators and network-on-chip(NoC). I have completed my B.Sc. from the department of Computer Science and Engineering, Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology, in April 2019. After that, I have worked as a software engineer at Kona Software Lab Ltd. Additionally, I worked as an undergraduate research student under the supervision of Dr. Muhammad Abdullah Adnan.
